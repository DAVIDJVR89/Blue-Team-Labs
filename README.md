# 🔵 Blue Team Labs Portfolio

Documenting my path into **SOC and Blue Team operations** while preparing for the **BTL1 (Blue Team Level 1)** certification. This repository covers hands-on practice in alert triage, log analysis, incident escalation, and containment.

My offensive background ([Pentesting-Labs](https://github.com/DAVIDJVR89/Pentesting-Labs), eJPTv2) plays a direct role here: understanding how attacks are executed makes it easier to recognize their footprint in logs and alerts, and respond to them faster.

## 📂 What's Inside

- **SOC Fundamentals:** Alert triage, severity prioritization, and SIEM dashboard practice.
- **Investigation:** IP/indicator analysis, cross-referencing with OSINT sources (e.g. WHOIS).
- **Response:** Incident escalation and containment actions (e.g. firewall blocking), documented with reasoning.

Each write-up focuses on process and reasoning rather than just the answer. The goal is to show how I think through an incident, not just that I solved it.
